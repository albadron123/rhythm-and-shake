Core pillars of "Rhythm & Shake":

-- Player communication
Each player should feel the desire to play this game not alone but his friends and I should support players with their will of communication.
It should be represented in game design as well as in the community building
-- Accurate feedback
In a rhythm game it's vital to show how well the person is playing and how and when he should improve, so it should be clear how the player
is performing
-- Player's creativity
It's important to give a player multiple ways to express himself. it the way he performs the level, in the way he shares the level, in the way he 
builds levels.
